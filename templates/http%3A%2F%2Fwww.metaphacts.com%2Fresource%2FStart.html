What is the metaphactory

metaphactory is an end-to-end platform for creating and utilizing enterprise knowledge graphs - from semantic graph data management to data-driven application development. It offers capabilities and features to support the entire lifecycle of dealing with knowledge graphs.

Its generic approach offers great flexibility in different usage scenarios and for various application areas. Knowledge workers benefit from the ability to create and gain meaningful insight into their data with one comprehensive software solution.
